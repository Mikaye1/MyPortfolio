Mikayel Abrahamyan comes from a long line of doctors in his family.
From 2002 to 2009, Abrahamyan studied at Yerevan State Medical University in Armenia and received his Master of Science in Medical Sciences degree.
After graduating he worked as a Primary Doctor and as a Physical Therapist, specializing in the spine. Abrahamyan has since been working on two inventions that cater to back pain stemming from herniated discs.
